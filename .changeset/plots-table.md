Add summary-table and diagnostics-heatmap views (terminal, SVG, and HTML). `summaryTableData` builds a full per-variable diagnostic row (mean, std, MCSE, quantiles, summed IMSE ESS, bulk/tail ESS, R-hat, classic split-R-hat, Geweke z, HDI); `diagnosticsHeatmapData` scores each variable across seven metrics and pre-colors the cells on a green/amber/red ramp.
