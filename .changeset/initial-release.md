Initial public release: the `Samples` data model and samples-file parsers
(`@mcmcjs/core`), MCMC convergence diagnostics — rank-normalized split-R-hat,
bulk/tail ESS, MCSE, and HDI (`@mcmcjs/diagnostics`), and the `mcmc diagnose`
command-line tool with human and `--json` output (`mcmcjs`).
