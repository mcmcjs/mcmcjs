Corner (pair) plots modeled on PairPlots.jl: cornerData builds an N x N grid with layered bodies (filtered scatter, hexbin, 2-D histogram, sigma-mass KDE contours, filled contours, trend lines, correlations, truth lines, bands) and diagonals (histogram, step histogram, KDE, quantile lines, median-with-errors titles), with ESS-driven bandwidths and bins, tiered defaults by series count, and SVG, terminal, and HTML renderers.
